A patient presented with symptoms including cough. The patient was diagnosed with malignant pleural mesothelioma, also described as a tumor and pleural mesothelioma. This diagnosis was linked to asbestos exposure.

Further investigation revealed the patient had malignant pleural mesothelioma, a type of cancer. The patient had a history of asbestos inhalation, which can lead to chronic inflammation. Genetic testing revealed germline mutations of BRCA 1 Associated Protein (BAP1). The patient also underwent ionizing radiation therapy. The patient experienced dyspnea, chest pain, weight loss, and fatigue.
