A 69-year-old male patient presented to the hospital in October 2018 with swollen lymph nodes in the right inguinal region for 7 months and dyssynergic defecation and constipation for 1 month. He also experienced symptoms of incomplete intestinal obstruction and vomiting, which aggravated when he took food. Reexamination revealed gallbladder obstruction and increased mesenteric nodules. The patient died 31.6 months later due to incomplete intestinal obstruction.
