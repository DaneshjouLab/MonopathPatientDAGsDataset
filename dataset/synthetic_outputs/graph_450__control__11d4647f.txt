A patient presented with a history of silicosis, lung cancer, and other non-malignant respiratory diseases. The patient initially exhibited respiratory symptoms.

Over time, the patient developed chronic bronchitis, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), emphysema, silicosis, and silico-tuberculosis. The patient was later diagnosed with chronic silicosis. Lung cancer was also diagnosed. The patient experienced COPD, silicosis, and airflow obstruction. The patient continued to experience respiratory symptoms.

The patient presented with cough and phlegm.
