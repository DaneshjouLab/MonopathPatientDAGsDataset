A patient presented with bone metastasis, experiencing pain, instability, and neurological deficits. The patient also suffered from pathological fractures, vertebral collapse, and vertebral compression fractures. Spinal fractures and metastatic bone lesions were also observed. The patient was diagnosed with myeloma. Further investigation revealed vertebral metastasis, contributing to the pathological fractures and vertebral compression fractures. The patient experienced multiple instances of vertebral compression fractures.
