A patient presented with non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), specifically adenocarcinoma, harboring an activating rearrangement of EML4-ALK. The patient also experienced multiple aseptic and recurrent abscesses. Further investigation revealed a tumorous formation on the right kidney and renal cyst formation. The patient reported symptoms of fatigue, inappetence, intermittent sweating, and diffuse abdominal pain. The patient also experienced infection.
